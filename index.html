ABOUT


Daniel Reeves


South Ogden, Utah


Graphic Designer, Artist, Photographer, Healthcare Proffessional


I want to solve your problems. Life has a way of confronting us with problems, and I take a lot of pride in my ability to solve problems because I have had to solve my own. Ever since I was young, I wanted to be an artist and a designer. When I got to college, I knew I could not mortgage my future by taking student loans. My solution to my problem was to get a job in the medical field as an EMT. I would go on to work in healthcare for eight years while earning a degree in Graphic Design from Salt Lake Community College. When I left school, I did so debt-free and with a wealth of knowledge from 3 jobs in multiple fields.Currently, I work as a Registered Polysomnographic Technologist at Intermountain Healthcare. I have been in this position for over 5 years, I have had an opportunity to solve many problems. I have gained skills as a builder of databases, show off my skills of design, and be an educator to over 30 employees. I have built several databases that solved problems posed by Covid-19 in 2020, such as building a database that made all paper-based surveys electronic in 7 days. After designing the database, I had to then educate an entire department on the new database I had just invented by creating instructions and giving lectures. These databases now cater to over 30,000 patients each year. I was once told I am a “Jack of all trades” meaning I had “Knowledge of many, but a master of none”. I was not aware until recently that a second line exists. The full saying is “Knowledge of many, a master of none, is better than a master of one”. I seek out problems to solve and take great pride in making where I am employed better. I am very goal-oriented and seek knowledge in all things especially those topics I am unfamiliar with. My personal credo each day is to learn and not merely to know.
